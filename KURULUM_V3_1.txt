FACTORYBOX v3.1 - DS18B20 SICAKLIK SENSORU

1) Bu ZIP icindeki dosyalari mevcut MiaDeviceOS proje ana klasorunun ustune kopyalayin.
2) Windows "Dosyalari hedefte degistir" sorusuna onay verin.
3) VS Code > PlatformIO > PROJECT TASKS > esp32dev > General > Build.
4) Build SUCCESS olursa Upload.
5) Upload SUCCESS olursa Monitor.

Beklenen seri port satirlari:
- MiaDeviceOS v3.1.0
- DS18B20 sensor bulundu.
- Sicaklik: xx.xx C

MQTT test topic:
mia/site01/laser01/command

MQTT test payload:
{"command":"get_temperature","request_id":"temperature-test-001"}

Cevap topic:
mia/site01/laser01/command/status
